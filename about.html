About Us

We are a dynamic duo of community and student empowerment initiatives in Lugari Constituency, Kakamega County, Western Kenya.

Lugari Youth Community

A platform dedicated to uniting and empowering the youth of Lugari Constituency. Our goal is to connect young people to explore opportunities, share ideas, and drive positive change in our community. Initiated by Hon. Nabii Nabwire, we focus on fostering a sense of belonging and opportunity among Lugari's youth.

Lugari Constituency comprises six wards:

  • Lumakanda
  • Lugari
  • Mautuma
  • Lwandeti
  • Chekalini
  • Chevaywa
