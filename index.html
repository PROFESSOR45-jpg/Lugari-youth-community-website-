Welcome to Lugari Youth Community

Lugari Youth Community is a platform dedicated to uniting the youth of Lugari Constituency, Kakamega County, Western Kenya. Our goal is to connect and empower young people to explore opportunities, share ideas, and drive positive change in our community.

Our Location

Lugari Constituency is located in Kakamega County, Western Kenya, and comprises of six wards:

  • Lumakanda
  • Lugari
  • Mautuma
  • Lwandeti
  • Chekalini
  • Chevaywa

Our Leadership

Our constituency is represented by Hon. Nabii Nabwire and other dedicated leaders (MCAs) who are committed to the development of our community.

Our Founder

The Lugari Youth Community was initiated by Hon. Nabii Nabwire, with the vision of empowering and connecting the youth of Lugari. Join us as we work together to build a brighter future for Lugari.
